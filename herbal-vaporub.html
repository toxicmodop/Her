How to Make Herbal VapoRub

Follow this step-by-step guide to craft your own 20 ml batch of natural VapoRub at home.

📋
Yields: 20 ml (approximately one small jar)
Equipment needed: Double boiler or heatproof bowl, small saucepan, glass stir rod or spoon, 20 ml glass jar, dropper or pipette, kitchen scale
Time: ~20 minutes + 30–45 minutes cooling time
1

🧹 Sanitize Your Equipment

Clean all tools and your glass jar with hot soapy water, then rinse with rubbing alcohol and allow to dry completely. Cleanliness prevents contamination and extends shelf life.

2

🐝 Melt the Beeswax

Place 3 gm of beeswax in your heatproof bowl over a double boiler (a bowl set over a pot of simmering water). Stir gently until completely melted. Beeswax melts at around 62–65°C.

💡 Tip: Use grated or pellet beeswax for faster, more even melting.
3

🥥 Add the Carrier Oils

Once beeswax is melted, add 8 ml coconut oil and 5 ml olive oil into the bowl. Stir continuously until all oils are fully combined and the mixture is smooth and uniform.

💡 Tip: If coconut oil is solid, it will melt quickly once added to the warm beeswax.
4

❄️ Add Camphor

Add 1 gm of camphor powder or crystals to the warm oil mixture while still on the double boiler. Stir well until completely dissolved. Camphor dissolves easily in warm oil.

⚠️ Safety: Camphor vapors can be strong — ensure good ventilation while mixing.
5

🌡️ Remove from Heat & Cool Slightly

Remove the bowl from the double boiler and allow the mixture to cool for 3–5 minutes. It should still be liquid but not too hot (around 45–50°C is ideal). This step preserves the therapeutic compounds in the essential oils.

6

🌿 Add Essential Oils

Add your essential oils one by one, stirring after each addition:

  • 🌿 Peppermint oil: 3–4 drops
  • 🌰 Clove oil: 2–3 drops
  • 🌲 Eucalyptus oil: 6–7 drops
💡 Tip: Add oils to cooler mixture to preserve their volatile therapeutic compounds.
7

🫙 Pour into Jar

Quickly but carefully pour the liquid mixture into your clean, dry 20 ml glass jar before it starts to solidify. Work efficiently as the mixture can set quickly, especially in cool environments.

💡 Tip: Warm the jar slightly with hot water first to slow setting and prevent cracking.
8

⏳ Allow to Set

Leave the jar uncovered at room temperature for 30–45 minutes until fully solidified. Do not refrigerate as this can cause the product to become too hard. Once set, close the lid tightly.

9

🏷️ Label & Store

Label your jar with the date of preparation and ingredients. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Shelf life is approximately 6–12 months when stored properly.
