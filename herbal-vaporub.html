Our Natural Ingredients

Every ingredient in our 20 ml jar is chosen for its proven therapeutic properties.

🐝

Beeswax

3 gm

The natural base of our formula. Beeswax provides the perfect texture, locks in moisture, creates a protective barrier on skin, and is naturally antibacterial. It solidifies the rub at room temperature while melting smoothly on contact with skin warmth.

🫒

Olive Oil

5 ml

Rich in antioxidants and fatty acids, olive oil deeply nourishes and softens the skin. It acts as an excellent carrier oil, helping other active ingredients penetrate the skin barrier and enhancing the overall spreadability of the rub.

🥥

Coconut Oil

8 ml

Our primary carrier oil. Coconut oil is naturally antimicrobial, deeply moisturizing, and has a pleasant light texture. It helps the rub absorb beautifully and keeps skin hydrated while allowing essential oils to work effectively.

❄️

Camphor

1 gm

A classic decongestant ingredient used for centuries. Camphor provides a cooling sensation, helps relieve congestion, soothes muscle aches, and creates the characteristic refreshing aroma associated with effective vapour rubs.

🌿

Peppermint Essential Oil

3–4 drops

Menthol-rich peppermint oil delivers an intense cooling and refreshing effect, helps open nasal passages, and provides a soothing sensation to the chest. It also has mild analgesic properties for temporary pain relief.

🌰

Clove Essential Oil

2–3 drops

Clove oil contains eugenol, a powerful natural compound with antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties. It adds warmth to the formula, helps fight respiratory infections, and enhances the overall therapeutic effect.

🌲

Eucalyptus Essential Oil

6–7 drops

The star decongestant of the formula. Eucalyptol (1,8-cineole) in eucalyptus oil is clinically shown to reduce airway mucus and ease breathing. It also has antimicrobial properties that help fight cold and flu viruses.

How to Make Herbal VapoRub

Follow this step-by-step guide to craft your own 20 ml batch of natural VapoRub at home.

📋
Yields: 20 ml (approximately one small jar)
Equipment needed: Double boiler or heatproof bowl, small saucepan, glass stir rod or spoon, 20 ml glass jar, dropper or pipette, kitchen scale
Time: ~20 minutes + 30–45 minutes cooling time
1

🧹 Sanitize Your Equipment

Clean all tools and your glass jar with hot soapy water, then rinse with rubbing alcohol and allow to dry completely. Cleanliness prevents contamination and extends shelf life.

2

🐝 Melt the Beeswax

Place 3 gm of beeswax in your heatproof bowl over a double boiler (a bowl set over a pot of simmering water). Stir gently until completely melted. Beeswax melts at around 62–65°C.

💡 Tip: Use grated or pellet beeswax for faster, more even melting.
3

🥥 Add the Carrier Oils

Once beeswax is melted, add 8 ml coconut oil and 5 ml olive oil into the bowl. Stir continuously until all oils are fully combined and the mixture is smooth and uniform.

💡 Tip: If coconut oil is solid, it will melt quickly once added to the warm beeswax.
4

❄️ Add Camphor

Add 1 gm of camphor powder or crystals to the warm oil mixture while still on the double boiler. Stir well until completely dissolved. Camphor dissolves easily in warm oil.

⚠️ Safety: Camphor vapors can be strong — ensure good ventilation while mixing.
5

🌡️ Remove from Heat & Cool Slightly

Remove the bowl from the double boiler and allow the mixture to cool for 3–5 minutes. It should still be liquid but not too hot (around 45–50°C is ideal). This step is important before adding essential oils to preserve their therapeutic compounds.

6

🌿 Add Essential Oils

Add your essential oils one by one, stirring after each addition:

  • 🌿 Peppermint oil: 3–4 drops
  • 🌰 Clove oil: 2–3 drops
  • 🌲 Eucalyptus oil: 6–7 drops
💡 Tip: Add oils to cooler mixture to preserve their volatile therapeutic compounds.
7

🫙 Pour into Jar

Quickly but carefully pour the liquid mixture into your clean, dry 20 ml glass jar before it starts to solidify. Work efficiently as the mixture can set quickly, especially in cool environments.

💡 Tip: Warm the jar slightly with hot water first to slow setting and prevent cracking.
8

⏳ Allow to Set

Leave the jar uncovered at room temperature for 30–45 minutes until fully solidified. Do not refrigerate as this can cause the product to become too hard. Once set, close the lid tightly.

9

🏷️ Label & Store

Label your jar with the date of preparation and ingredients. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Shelf life is approximately 6–12 months when stored properly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our Herbal VapoRub.

What is Herbal VapoRub and how does it differ from regular Vicks? +
Our Herbal VapoRub is a 100% natural alternative to conventional petroleum-based chest rubs. Unlike Vicks VapoRub which uses a petrolatum (petroleum jelly) base, our formula uses natural beeswax, coconut oil, and olive oil. We use pure essential oils instead of synthetic fragrances, and avoid all preservatives, colorants, and artificial additives.
Is it safe for babies and young children? +
Children under 2 years old: We strongly advise AGAINST use. Camphor and menthol (from peppermint) can be harmful to very young children and may cause breathing difficulties. For children aged 2–12: Use with caution and consult a pediatrician first. Apply only to the chest and back — never near the face, nose, or mouth. Always use a very small amount. For children over 12 and adults: Generally safe as directed. If in doubt, consult your healthcare provider.
How long does one 20 ml jar last? +
With regular daily use (once per day), a 20 ml jar typically lasts 2–4 weeks, as you only need a pea-sized amount per application. If used only during colds or congestion episodes, the jar can last several months. Once opened, use within 6 months for best potency of essential oils.
What is the shelf life of the product? +
Unopened, our Herbal VapoRub has a shelf life of 12 months stored in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Once opened, use within 6 months for optimal fragrance and therapeutic potency. The beeswax and oils naturally resist rancidity, but essential oils can lose potency over time. Always check for any change in smell or texture before use.
Where should I apply Herbal VapoRub? +
Apply to the chest and upper back for respiratory relief. It can also be applied to the neck (under the chin area) for throat and nasal congestion. For muscle aches, apply to the affected muscle area. Always avoid: eyes, inside the nose, near mouth, broken or irritated skin, and mucous membranes. Never ingest.
Can I use it during pregnancy? +
Pregnant and breastfeeding women should consult their healthcare provider before use. Camphor and some essential oils (including clove and peppermint in large amounts) may have contraindications during pregnancy. External topical application in very small amounts is generally considered low-risk, but medical guidance is always recommended during pregnancy.
How is the product packaged and shipped? +
Each 20 ml jar is packed in a clean glass jar with a secure screw-top lid, wrapped in protective bubble wrap, and shipped in a sturdy cardboard box. Orders are dispatched within 1–2 business days. We use eco-friendly packaging materials wherever possible. A product label with ingredients and usage instructions is included.
Can I use this for aromatherapy or diffusing? +
Our VapoRub is formulated specifically for topical application, not for diffusers. However, you can place the open jar near a bedside table to enjoy gentle aromatherapy as the natural vapors release at room temperature. For active diffusing, we recommend using pure eucalyptus or peppermint essential oils diluted in water in a standard ultrasonic diffuser.
Is the product vegan? +
Our formula contains beeswax, which is an animal-derived product, making it not strictly vegan but it is vegetarian. For those who follow a vegan lifestyle, we acknowledge that beeswax is an animal byproduct. All other ingredients in our formula are 100% plant-based. We are always researching vegan alternatives such as candelilla wax for future product variations.
What if I have a skin reaction or sensitivity? +
Perform a patch test before first use: apply a small amount to the inner wrist or elbow and wait 24 hours. If you experience redness, itching, burning, or irritation, discontinue use immediately and wash the area with soap and water. People with sensitivities to tree nuts (coconut) or bee products (beeswax) should exercise extra caution. If a severe reaction occurs, seek medical attention.

Safety Information

Your safety is our top priority. Please read all guidelines before use.

🛡️

Use Safely, Breathe Freely

Our Herbal VapoRub is crafted from natural ingredients and is generally safe for adults and children over 2 years when used as directed. However, natural does not always mean risk-free for everyone. Essential oils are highly concentrated and must be treated with respect. Please read all safety information before first use and keep this product out of reach of children.

🚫 Do NOT Use On / For

  • Children under 2 years old
  • Inside the nose, mouth, or ears
  • Directly on the face of infants
  • Broken, cut, or irritated skin
  • Open wounds or rashes
  • Near eyes or mucous membranes
  • Ingestion — for external use only

⚠️ Use with Caution

  • Children aged 2–12 (small amounts only)
  • Pregnant or breastfeeding women
  • People with asthma or respiratory conditions
  • Individuals with epilepsy (camphor alert)
  • Known allergy to beeswax or tree nuts
  • Sensitive or reactive skin types

✅ Safe Practices

  • Always do a patch test before first use
  • Apply only to chest, back, and neck
  • Use pea-sized amount per application
  • Wash hands after application
  • Keep jar tightly closed when not in use
  • Store away from heat and direct sunlight
  • Use within 6 months of opening

ℹ️ Camphor Safety Notice

  • Camphor can be toxic if ingested
  • Even small oral doses are dangerous for children
  • Keep all camphor products locked away from kids
  • Do not apply near infant's face or nose
  • Camphor vapors should not be directly inhaled by infants
  • If accidentally ingested, call Poison Control immediately

🌿 Essential Oil Safety

  • Pure essential oils are highly concentrated
  • Eucalyptus can be toxic in large amounts internally
  • Peppermint (menthol) may cause breathing issues in infants
  • Clove oil is a skin sensitizer — always diluted in our formula
  • Do not apply undiluted essential oils directly to skin
  • Keep away from pets, especially cats

🏥 When to Seek Medical Help

  • Severe skin reaction or chemical burn
  • Product contact with eyes (flush with water, see doctor)
  • Accidental ingestion — call Poison Control immediately
  • Respiratory distress after use
  • Allergic reaction (hives, swelling, difficulty breathing)
  • Persistent rash or irritation
